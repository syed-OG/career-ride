How to Use Roadmaps
DO
  • Use roadmaps as a guide, not as a rule book
  • Focus on understanding the concepts, not just checking off items
  • Build projects along the way to apply your learning
  • Adapt the roadmap based on your specific career goals
  • Choose depth over breadth for the technologies that matter most for your goals
DON'T
  • Follow roadmaps blindly without understanding your personal goals
  • Try to learn everything at once, focus on one area at a time
  • Get caught in tutorial hell - build real projects to apply skills
  • Skip fundamentals in favor of trendy frameworks
  • Feel overwhelmed - progress over perfection
Remember: These roadmaps are meant to be a guide and not a definitive path. Adapt them to your needs and interests.
